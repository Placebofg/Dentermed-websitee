About Dentermed

Our journey began with a simple belief: talent is universal, but opportunity is not.

Our Mission

Founded in 2015 by a group of medical professionals who overcame similar challenges, Dentermed is dedicated to leveling the playing field for students from underserved communities who aspire to careers in medicine and dentistry.

We've grown from mentoring 5 students in our first year to supporting over 500 annually across 12 states, with a 92% success rate in helping our mentees gain admission to medical or dental programs.

What makes us unique is our holistic approach - we don't just help with applications, we provide ongoing support through mentorship, skill-building workshops, and financial guidance to ensure long-term success.
